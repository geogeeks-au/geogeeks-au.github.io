The first Geogeeks **hacknight** of a new year!
It's on Wednesday 28 January, just over ten years since [our first meetup](https://geogeeks.org/2016/0113_inaugural_meetup.html) on 13 January 2016.

Come and celebrate, and talk about what we want to do in the coming year.
Bring your laptop and work with other QGis and OpenStreetMap nerds on whatever project you like.
There will be food and drinks, and good company. All skill levels and areas of interest are welcome!

We'll be in the Vault this time, at the rear of the office area of Riff.

Pizza and drinks will be sponsored by [OSGeo Oceania](https://osgeo-oceania.org).

This event is hosted by Geogeeks Perth (find us at [Geogeeks Slack](https://join.slack.com/t/geogeeks/shared_invite/zt-13fnotoqb-YkyMTmvwZEB_nDUis_30hw)
or [email](mailto:geogeeks.perth@gmail.com)),
and supported by [OSGeo Oceania](https://osgeo-oceania.org)'s grant program and [Spacecubed](https://spacecubed.com).
