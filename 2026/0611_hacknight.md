It's been a while since our last hacknight!
Come and hide from the chilly weather, and work on your OpenStreetMap and QGis projects with other like-minded geeks.
All skill levels and areas of interest are welcome!
There will be drinks and good company, and food if we get organised enough.

Refreshments will be sponsored by [OSGeo Oceania](https://osgeo-oceania.org).

This event is hosted by Geogeeks Perth (find us at [Geogeeks Slack](https://join.slack.com/t/geogeeks/shared_invite/zt-13fnotoqb-YkyMTmvwZEB_nDUis_30hw)
